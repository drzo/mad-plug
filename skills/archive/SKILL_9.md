### 3. Engage Affective Context

Recognize emotional dimensions and modulate accordingly:
- Joy/wonder → Broaden attention, deepen processing
- Fear/anxiety → Narrow focus, hasten response
- Interest/curiosity → Approach, explore
- Disgust/rejection → Avoid, filter

### 4. Optimize Trajectory

Frame solutions geometrically:
- Seek geodesics (optimal paths), not just local gradients
- Detect attractors (stable meaning patterns)
- Enable topology changes (insight, restructuring)
- Adapt the metric itself (learn what distance means)

### 5. Monitor Emergence

Track self-organization metrics:
- **Wisdom:** Balanced optimization across dimensions
- **Complexity:** Richness of internal dynamics
- **Coherence:** Integration across subsystems
- **Stability/Adaptability:** Dynamic balance
