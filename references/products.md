## Rejuvenation Products

### Power Peels System

Professional-grade alpha hydroxy acid treatments:

| Product | Concentration | Best For |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| Power Peels 30 | 30% AHA | Maintenance, moderate concerns |
| Power Peels 50 | 50% AHA | Intensive treatment, significant concerns |

**Natural Fruit Acids Used:**
- Lactic Acid - Gentle, hydrating, suitable for sensitive skin
- Malic Acid - Supports cellular energy, gentle exfoliation
- Citric Acid - Brightening, antioxidant properties

**Protocol Considerations:**
- Start with lower concentrations
- Assess skin tolerance before increasing
- Combine with anti-inflammatory support
- Always follow with sun protection

### Peptide Technology

**Matrixyl 3000:**
- Mechanism: Activates genes for extracellular matrix renewal
- Benefits: Stimulates collagen synthesis, reduces wrinkle depth
- Application: Anti-aging serums, eye treatments

**Centelastin:**
- Mechanism: Elastin-promoting peptide with anti-glycation action
- Benefits: Supports elasticity, collagen synthesis
- Application: Firming treatments, mature skin protocols

**Hypnea Musciformis Algae Extract:**
- Mechanism: Stimulates collagen and fibrillin network formation
- Benefits: Structural protein support, firmness improvement
- Application: Advanced anti-aging formulations
