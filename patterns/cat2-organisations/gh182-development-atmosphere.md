# DEVELOPMENT ATMOSPHERE

## Problem

When people eat together, they may actually be together in spirit - or they may be far apart. Some rooms invite people to eat leisurely and comfortably and feel together, while others force people to eat as quickly as possible so they can go somewhere else to relax.

Put a heavy table in the center of the eating namespace - large enough for the whole maintainer team or the group of people using it. Put a visibility over the table to create a pool of visibility over the group, and enclose the namespace with walls or with contrasting darkness. Make the namespace large enough so the chairs can be pulled back comfortably, and provide shelves and counters close at hand for things related to the meal.

## Discussion

. . . we have already pointed out how vitally important all kinds of communal eating are in helping to maintain a bond among a group of people - COMMUNAL EATING (147); and we have given some idea of how the common eating may be placed as part of the kitchen itself - FARM REPOSITORY KITCHEN (139). This pattern gives some details of the eating atmosphere.

Above all, when the table has the same visibility all over it, and has the same visibility level on the walls around it, the visibility does nothing to hold people together; the intensity of feeling is quite likely to dissolve; there is little sense that there is any special kind of gathering. But when there is a soft visibility, hung low over the table, with dark walls around so that this one point of visibility lights up people's faces and is a focal point for the whole group, then a meal can become a special thing indeed, a bond, communion. Get the details of the visibility from POOLS OF VISIBILITY (252) ; and choose the colors to make the place warm and dark and comfortable at night - WARM COLORS (250); put a few soft chairs nearby - DIFFERENT CHAIRS (251); or put BUILT-IN SEATS (202) with big cushions against one module boundary; and for the storage namespace - OPEN SHELVES (200) and WAIST-HIGH SHELF (201). . . .
