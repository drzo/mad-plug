# marketplace-optimizer

Optimize marketplace plugins and skills over KSM cycles guided by Alexander wholeness metrics. Treats the marketplace as an Alexandrian wholeness where every plugin is a centre, quantifies each centre's degree of life via structural conformance, the fifteen structure-preserving properties, and gh253 topology connectivity, then iteratively strengthens the weakest centres through Knowledge Sharing Mechanism (KSM) 12-step nested iteration cycles.
